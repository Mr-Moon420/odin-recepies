Wife Special Birthday Pain Bacon

Description

Imagine that you are the biggest drug chef in the world, but you family hates you, you can now only remember what once was and feel the pain of what you have lost.

Ingredients

  • Drug Empire
  • A Family You Broke
  • An Ex-Student that you used to once have fun with, still have respect for, but destroyed his life for your ego
  • Police on the run for you
  • Egg and Bacon

Steps

  1. Order egg and bacon on your birthday.
  2. Use a fake ID
  3. Arrange your bacon in a way someone you loved used to
  4. Remember what once was
  5. Set out for revenge
